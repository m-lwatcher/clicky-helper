# Quirky Help Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-15_

Quirky Help is a Windows desktop app that helps users understand screenshots, terminal errors, setup issues, and command suggestions.

This policy explains what information the app uses, where it is stored, and when it may be sent to an AI provider.

## Summary

Quirky Help does not auto-run commands, make system changes, collect analytics, or operate a separate backend service.

The app uses the AI provider selected by the user. Prompts and screenshots are sent only to the provider configured in the app settings.

## Information stored locally

Quirky Help may store the following settings locally on your device:

- selected AI provider
- API key
- model name
- provider base URL
- onboarding/setup completion state

These settings are stored locally so the app can reopen ready to use. They are not sent to the developer by Quirky Help.

## Information sent to AI providers

When you ask Quirky Help a question, the app may send the following to your configured AI provider:

- the prompt or question you typed
- any screenshot or image you attached
- related request settings needed to generate the answer

The specific provider depends on what you configure in the app, such as Gemini, Groq, OpenAI, or a custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

The selected AI provider may process and store data according to its own privacy policy and terms. Review your chosen provider's policies before using the app with sensitive information.

## Screenshots and sensitive information

Screenshots can contain private or sensitive information, including:

- account details
- file paths
- API keys or tokens
- wallet addresses
- private messages
- personal documents
- browser tabs or URLs

Review screenshots before sending them through the app. Do not attach screenshots containing sensitive information unless you are comfortable sending that information to your configured AI provider.

## Commands and system changes

Quirky Help does not automatically run commands or make changes to your computer.

The app may suggest PowerShell, CMD, or other commands. These are suggestions only. Users are responsible for reviewing commands before copying or running them.

## Analytics and tracking

Quirky Help does not include built-in analytics, advertising trackers, or telemetry in this version.

## Developer access

The developer does not receive your prompts, screenshots, API keys, or provider settings through a separate Quirky Help backend.

If you manually share logs, screenshots, or support details with the developer, that information will be used only to help troubleshoot the issue you reported.

## Third-party services

Quirky Help can connect to third-party AI providers selected by the user. These providers may include, but are not limited to:

- Google Gemini
- Groq
- OpenAI
- custom OpenAI-compatible providers

Use of those providers is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

## Children's privacy

Quirky Help is not designed to collect information from children. The app is intended as a general productivity and troubleshooting tool.

## Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated as Quirky Help changes. The updated date at the top of this document will reflect the latest revision.

## Contact

For support, contact: support_quirkyhelp@proton.me

Do not send API keys, private tokens, or sensitive screenshots in plain text unless you are comfortable sharing that information for troubleshooting.
